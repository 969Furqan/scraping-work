English Language Requirements for all Undergraduate and Diploma/Certificate Programs

Entry Requirements for all Programs

Applicants from Pakistan are required to submit Intermediate/Higher Secondary School Certificate with a minimum overall grade of 50%. A minimum grade of 50% is required for all required subject/s.
