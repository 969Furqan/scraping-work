Objectives
Young journalists who have graduated with the Master's degree are able to work in the various professional and technological environments to be found in the jobs market: daily and periodical press, current and in-depth television or radio, web, blogs and multimedia channels.
At the end of a hard two-year period of study and work, the journalistic profile is that of a professional who knows the work of the news, of the editorial staff, of the management of social channels and of the new platforms of digital distribution of highly journalistic contents, which understands the needs of the publishing company and of the market, in the awareness of the social responsibility of the journalistic profession.

Target
The Master is intended for first and second level graduates from the old degree system at all Italian universities and to graduates of legally recognised foreign universities.
