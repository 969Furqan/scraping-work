Presentation
Tuition Fees
The master, in collaboration with Sky, is the only course in Italy providing participants with the know-how to design and produce programs and television formats dedicated to cinema and series, from the daily column to the big live event.
Through the testimonials of experts (university lecturers, managers, journalists, art directors, authors, film critics, producers, directors), case histories, practical workshops and guided tours of Sky's recording studios, it will be possible to acquire the basic techniques and useful tools to:
understand the mechanisms of linear and on-demand programming on audiovisual platforms
understand the logic that govern the programming of satellite channels specializing in cinema and seriality
analyze their communication strategies, including on the web and social media
Thematic areas:
Linear and on-demand programming
Communication and merchandising
Editorial staff
TV Author
Production
Direction and post-production
Practical workshops:
creating a cinema format. Build the lineup of a film program
conducting and interviewing/conducting tests for an interview
budget of a program/event, production plan and organization of a program/event
Following the direction of an event / TV program
finalization of a TV product
editing and directing a trailer/promo
Possible job opportunities include positions at television and broadcaster networks, production houses, communication agencies, public relations agencies and press offices.
