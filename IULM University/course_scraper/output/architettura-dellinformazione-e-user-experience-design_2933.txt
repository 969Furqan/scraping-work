The Master's course in Information Architecture & User Experience Design is the course of study designed to meet the growing demand for professionals in the digital revolution. Its specific mission is to train specialists who are able to design sites, desktop and mobile applications, intranets, to manage digital communication for businesses and public administration and to organize complex amount of information.
Lectures are delivered in
blended
formula: online and on campus.
The course can also be entirely delivered in
online
formula.
Thematic areas:
Ethnographic research
Information architecture
Codesign
Crosschannel User Experience
Fundamentals of visual design
Fundamentals of cognitive psychology
Interaction design
Functional Design
Rapid prototyping techniques
Fundamentals of data modeling
Fundamentals of Project management
Search Engine Optimization (SEO)
Fundamentals of Digital Marketing
Introduction to markup languages ​​and coding fundamentals
Fundamentals of Artificial Intelligence
Social media design
Test with users
Language
Italian

Target
University students and communication professionals who need to learn the dynamics of digital information, graphic designers, developers and project managers who want to broaden their skill set.
The Master's course is open to all those who need to acquire more knowledge to make their digital business more effective.
