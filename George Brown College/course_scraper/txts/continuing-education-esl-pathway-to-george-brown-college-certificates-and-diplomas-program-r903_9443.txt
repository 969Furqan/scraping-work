Program Description
Program Overview
Continuing Education ESL Pathway to George Brown College Certificates and Diplomas
will help you meet the minimum English Language admissions requirement for any of George Brown's full-time Ontario College Certificate or Diploma programs. This pathway is available within George Brown College only.
Full Description
The Continuing Education ESL Pathway to George Brown College Certificates and Diplomas program
will help you transition your studies from a part-time continuing education program into one of our exciting full-time programs.
After successfully completing this pathway program, you'll have met the minimum English Language admissions requirement for any of George Brown's full-time Ontario College Certificate or Diploma programs.
Continuing Education ESL Pathway to George Brown College Certificates and Diplomas Program Details:
Completion of this program will eliminate the need for you to write TOEFL, IELTS or George Brown's English Admissions Assessment.
George Brown's Admissions Department will recognize these pathway courses as the English Language requirement for any George Brown full-time Certificate or Diploma programs.
This pathway only applies while studying at George Brown and not at other institutions.
Completing this pathway program doesn't guarantee entry to one of George Brown's certificate or diploma programs––you'll still be ranked with all other applicants for your program(s) of choice, and you'll need to meet any other requirements for your specific program.
PLEASE NOTE:
Information related to courses, including length, dates, times, location and delivery mode are subject to change without notice.
