Program Description
Program Overview
George Brown College’s English for Professional Success Certificate
will give you the English language skills you need to succeed in Canada's highly competitive workplace. By polishing your verbal, listening and written communication skills, this part-time program will build your confidence in personal and professional interactions. If you’re a non-native speaker and have advanced-level English skills, this program is for you.
Please note
: Classes are delivered in-person during evenings and weekends to accommodate working professionals.
Full Description
George Brown College’s English for Professional Success Certificate
is a part-time program for those looking to take their English language skills to the next level. As part of this certificate, you must complete three core courses and your choice of two electives.
Details for George Brown's English for Professional Success Certificate Program:
This part-time program offers classes on evenings and weekends.
This program is intended for non-native speakers with advanced-level English skills.
Ideal for working professionals looking to build confidence with personal and professional cross-cultural communications.
You can take courses for this program in whatever order you prefer.
Please note:
Information related to courses, including length, dates, times, location and delivery mode are subject to change without notice.
Alumni Impact
We are immensely proud of the contributions of our alumni in Toronto and around the globe.
From Michelin-starred restaurants to major construction, entertainment, community and financial organizations, our graduates are truly making an impact across a range of industries.

Admission Requirements
Apply to this program if you are interested in further developing your verbal, listening and written communication English language skills for both personal and professional use.
As this is an advanced-level certificate, you must complete
ESL 9068 (ESL IIIB Advanced)
or have an equivalent English placement test result prior to taking the core courses in this certificate.
Prior to enrolling in any of the elective courses, you must complete
ESL 9067 (ESL IIIA Low Advanced)
or have the equivalent English placement test.
Special Requirements
You must achieve a grade of B (73 per cent) or better to successfully complete each course.
To achieve this advanced-level certificate, you must complete three core courses and your choice of two electives.
Placement Test
If you are writing the level placement test for program entry, please visit
Continuing Education (part–time) ESL Program Placement Test Registration
to sign up for the test after you have registered for the program.
