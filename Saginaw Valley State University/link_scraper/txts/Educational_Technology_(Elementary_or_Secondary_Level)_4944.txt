Educational Technology (Elementary or Secondary Level)
Endorsement:
Educational Technology
Endorsement Code:
NP
Available To:
Elementary-level Certified Teachers
Secondary-level Certified Teachers
Description of Endorsement:
The Educational Technology (NP) endorsement allows elementary and secondary-level teachers to use technology resources for their own class instruction and for student learning classes for which they hold other endorsements. They will be well-prepared to teach computers, computer literacy, word processing, keyboarding, computer applications, web design, and introduction to the Internet. The College of Education will recommend teachers for the Educational Technology endorsement upon successful completion of all required coursework.
All coursework toward a graduate degree must be completed within a seven year period.
