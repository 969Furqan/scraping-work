About
The Entrepreneurship program is designed to prepare future entrepreneurs with the skills and knowledge to successfully start their own businesses and engage in successful corporate entrepreneurship.
Students learn about the strategic aspects of the entrepreneurial process beginning with the identification of business opportunities, the development, and validation of unique business models that meet targeted consumer needs, the formation of the entrepreneurial team, securing talent, financial and other resources, and the ethical and social responsibilities needed for entrepreneurial success.
