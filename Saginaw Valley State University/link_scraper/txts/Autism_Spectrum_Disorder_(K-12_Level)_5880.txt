Endorsement:
Autism Spectrum Disorder
Endorsement Code:
SV
Available To:
Elementary-level Certified Teachers
Secondary-level Certified Teachers
Description of Endorsement:
The Autism Spectrum Disorder (SV) endorsement allows elementary and secondary-level certified teachers to teach the entire K-12 curriculum to their students who receive special education services. The College of Education will recommend teachers for the K-12 Autism Spectrum Disorder endorsement upon successful completion of all required coursework and the Michigan Test for Teacher Certification (MTTC) Autism Spectrum Disorder subject-area test: secondary-level certified teachers must also pass the MTTC Elementary Education Test.
(The 500 and 600-level courses required for this endorsement may be used toward the Master of Arts in Teaching - Special Education degree program. All coursework toward a graduate degree must be completed within a seven year period.)
Associated MTTC Test:
064 Autism Spectrum Disorder
(Students should indicate SVSU Institution Code #24 when registering for the MTTC test.)

To add this endorsement to an existing teaching certificate select the following link for a list of required courses.
