A.S. Health Science
Track 1 is for students pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Health Science (BSHS), Nursing, Dental Hygiene, Radiographic Science, or Communication Science and Disorders programs.
Track 2 is for students pursuing the Medical Lab Science, Pharmacy, or other graduate-level health profession. (PA, PT, OT, Medical, Dental, etc.)
Program Admission Requirements
There are no program admission requirements for the A.S. in Health Science.
