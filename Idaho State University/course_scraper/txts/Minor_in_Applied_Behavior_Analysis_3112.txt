Minor in Applied Behavior Analysis
The Minor in Applied Behavior Analysis serves as the academic coursework component for students seeking to become certified as a Board Certified Assistant Behavior Analyst (BCaBA).  This program will provide students with the foundational knowledge of the field of behavior analysis including concepts, principles, measurement and experimental design, as well as the development of skills in behavior assessment, intervention planning, and ethical considerations.
