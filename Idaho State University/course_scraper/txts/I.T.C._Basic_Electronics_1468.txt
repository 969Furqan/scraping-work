I.T.C. Basic Electronics
(1 Year)
Program Objectives
Obtain gainful employment as electronics technicians and technical support personnel capable of working in a wide variety of electronics-related fields.
Continue to expand their knowledge and remain current in a continuously expanding industry.
Successfully integrate as productive team members in the electronics industry utilizing written, oral and electronic communications skills.
