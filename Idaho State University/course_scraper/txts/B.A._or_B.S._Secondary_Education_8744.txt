Summary of Requirements for a Bachelor of Arts or a Bachelor
of Science degree in Secondary Education:
Completion of University's General Education Requirements (see
General Education Requirements
and
Applying to Graduate
in the Academic Information
section of this catalog).

Education Core, and the Required Secondary Education Course, listed below.
