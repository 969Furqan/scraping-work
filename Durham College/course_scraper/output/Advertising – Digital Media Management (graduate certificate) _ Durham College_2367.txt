Program Details
Designed for students with previous education and/or experience in advertising and promotional communication, this program broadens understanding of integrated campaigns, while teaching new skills in strategic planning, content creation, research, design, and implementation of digital programs. Students solve real-world challenges for clients utilizing digital engagement and activation strategies that help achieve specific advertising and marketing objectives.
The integration of artificial intelligence (AI) into these strategies ensures students are at the forefront of digital marketing innovation.
You will learn how to:
Develop and defend digital media plans as part of a client’s overall marketing communications, including integrated plans with paid, owned and earned strategies and tactics.
Explore effective digital strategy options, such as SEO, search, social, email, influencer, affiliate marketing and more.
Cultivate a community with content designed for engagement and connection.
Conceptualize and produce digital advertising materials, including standard online ad formats, posts, blogs, advertorials and other brand integrations.
Investigate popular digital analytics tools used to monitor and evaluate consumer media and buying behaviour.
Through experiential learning opportunities with our on-campus ad agency,
Collaboragency
, and the use of industry-leading tools and AI technology, students gain hands-on experience that prepares them for success in the dynamic field of digital media.
Program Learning Outcomes
Resolve specific advertising and marketing communications challenges.
Develop, persuasively present and defend a professional advertising and marketing communications media plan based on research evidence.
Manage the execution of a digital advertising and marketing communications’ media plan applying project management principles.
Evaluate the effectiveness of a digital advertising and marketing communications’ initiative using industry monitoring and tracking tool.
Identify and adhere to digital advertising and marketing communications’ standards and practices in accordance with relevant Canadian Legislation.
Complete all work in a professional, ethical and timely manner.
Analyze historical and current issues and emerging trends affecting advertising and marketing communications.
Evaluate various digital strategies and media tactics to facilitate the execution of an effective integrated communications plan.
Conceive, write, design, edit, and produce digital advertising content utilizing industry-standard technologies.
Plan strategies for professional development to aid in the creation of an effective personal, digital online brand identity.

Experiential Learning
Collaboragency
Collaboragency is a fully integrated onsite communications agency at Durham College. The collaborators are a multi-disciplinary team of students from the Advertising, Graphic Design, Photography, Video Production programs and more. Together, students tackle projects for real clients looking to improve their businesses. From web, social media and marketing content creation to strategic planning, Collaboragency creates unique business solutions for our local entrepreneurs. Through the production of both strategic and creative materials, students can leverage work in portfolios when applying for desired occupations. This work also allows students to forge lasting connections with clients, who one day may be responsible for launching careers.

Advanced Tools and Technology in Our Curriculum
Our Advertising and Digital Media Management program integrates industry-standard tools to provide students with hands-on learning experiences similar to what you would encounter in your future career.
Some examples include:
Monday.com
:
Students use this project management platform to organize and execute tasks collaboratively, sharpening project management skills essential for media and marketing roles.
Vividata
:
With Vividata’s audience insights, students conduct market research, gaining experience in media planning and analytics—critical skills in the industry.
Semrush for Education
:
Through Semrush, students gain hands-on experience with SEO, content marketing, and social media analytics, earning valuable certifications that enhance their career prospects.
Stukent Mimic Social
:
This simulation tool provides students with an immersive experience in managing social media campaigns, allowing them to experiment with budgets, strategy, and content in a risk-free environment that mimics real-world social media advertising dynamics.
By incorporating these industry-standard tools into daily learning, graduates are prepared with the skills and experience demanded in today’s digital marketing landscape.
