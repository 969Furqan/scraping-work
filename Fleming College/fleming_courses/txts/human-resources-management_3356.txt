Human Resources Management
Consider a career in the rewarding field of Human Resource Management.
Program Highlights
Our postgraduate certificate program in Human Resources Management is strategically designed to give you the skillset and work experience necessary to launch your career in Human Resources.
Learn from practicing HR instructors and fulfill coursework requirements towards your Certified Human Resources Professional (CHRP) designation, overseen by the Human Resources Professionals Association (HRPA). This means you'll graduate qualified and ready to lead in the day-to-day activities of a Human Resources professional.
Is This You?
Human Resources is the perfect vocation for your interest in teamwork and tactful communication, your problem solving skills and your passion for policies and processes.
Other qualities it takes to succeed include:
A passion for people
Cross-cultural awareness
Communication skills: verbal, written and presentation
Ability to work in teams, assume leadership roles when required
Organization and time management skills
Your own personal laptop in and outside of the classroom. See technology requirements below for specifications
