Non-Credit Certificates at UNO
University of New Orleans offers certificate programs in a variety of fields. UNO certificates helps students expand their resumes, increase their skills in specific areas to advance in their job and career, and in some cases, fulfill statewide certificate requirements.
Whether you’re looking to start higher education, complete a certificate or training, or gain valuable skills to help you advance in your career, UNO Division of Professional and Continuing Education (PaCE) has the program for you.
