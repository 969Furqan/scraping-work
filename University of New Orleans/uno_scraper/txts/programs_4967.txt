Pre-Medical Laboratory Sciences
UNO offers the prerequisite courses designed to prepare students for admission to the Department of Medical Laboratory Sciences, LSU Health Sciences Center. Admission is on a competitive basis. Students who successfully complete all requirements will earn a Bachelor of Science in Medical Laboratory Sciences awarded by the Louisiana State University Health Sciences Center. Additional information about the pre-medical laboratory sciences curriculum may be obtained in the office of the College of Sciences (1100 Science Building).
