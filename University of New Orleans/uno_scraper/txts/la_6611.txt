UNO International Studies program
New Orleans, shaped since its founding by a host of global forces, is a rich site for studying our increasingly-linked world.
Students majoring in International Studies -- through UNO's interdisciplinary course offerings, internships, study abroad and other opportunities -- go global in the classroom and beyond!

Major in International Studies!
Students who earn bachelor's degrees in International Studies become more knowledgeable, skilled and engaged global citizens, and emerge from the program prepared to pursue careers in a host of international fields.  Students fulfill core internationally-oriented requirements and a focused, interdisciplinary curriculum, drawing primarily on coursework in Anthropology, Business, Economics, Urban Studies, English, Fine Arts, Geography, History, Philosophy, Political Science, Sociology, and Foreign Languages. Many also study abroad during their undergraduate careers, and most pursue an internship with an internationally-relevant organization of some kind.
