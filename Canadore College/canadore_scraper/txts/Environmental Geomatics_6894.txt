Environmental Geomatics
Train hands-on in the field and lab
Overview
Upcoming Intake:
Fall 2024, College Drive - North Bay
Enhance your training and employability with this specialized geomatics program. Learn applied geomatics technologies and methods including Geographic Information Systems (GIS), data visualization techniques, spatial modelling, and remote sensing and image analysis (from drones, aircraft, and satellites). Geomatics technologies are used to solve real-world challenges in numerous sectors including forestry, environmental, public health, agriculture, mining and more. Gain the skills you need to succeed in this in-demand career field.
