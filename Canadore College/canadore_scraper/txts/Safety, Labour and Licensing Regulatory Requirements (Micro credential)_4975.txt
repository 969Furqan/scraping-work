Safety, Labour and Licensing Regulatory Requirements (Micro credential)
Overview
This 12 hour Safety, Labour & Licensing Regulatory Requirements micro-credential examines building construction business operations as required by the Ontario Workplace Safety & Insurance Board (WSIB) and Canada Revenue Agency (CRA). Students identify the stages for planning for permits and inspections and keeping records in compliance with government regulations. Emphasis is on on health and safety regulations, and maintenance of employment records to comply with CRA requirements for employment reporting periods.
