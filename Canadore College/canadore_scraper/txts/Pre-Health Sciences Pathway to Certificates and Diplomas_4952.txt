Overview
Upcoming Intakes:
Fall 2024: College Drive – North Bay
Winter 2025: College Drive – North Bay
Obtain the knowledge you need to make an informed program choice, and the necessary skills and prerequisites to start your academic career in the health sector. This program will prepare you for further college studies in the health sciences sector.
Graduates of this program are prepared to meet the eligibility requirements for a number of Canadore College programs including: Practical Nursing, Biotechnology Technologist, Occupational Therapist Assistant and Physiotherapist Assistant, and Environment Technician - Protection and Compliance
Please note
that certain programs will require a minimum program GPA or minimum grades in specific courses
.
The Pre-Health Sciences Certificate at Canadore College is a pathway to certificate, diploma and advanced diploma programs at Canadore or within the college system.
