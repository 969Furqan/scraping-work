Early Childhood Education Intensive (accelerated)
Expand your knowledge and skill-set working in real-world settings.
Overview
Upcoming Intake:
Fall 2024: College Drive – North Bay
Graduates of any Bachelor of Education or Bachelor of Arts program can complete this hands-on, intensive program in one year.  Expand your knowledge and skill set working in real-world settings. A 175-hour field placement gives you a practical application for your training with the possibility of securing permanent employment.
Your Future Career
Childcare centres or home day care
Full-day kindergarten programs
Educational settings as educational assistants
Resource and learning access programs
Specialty early learning schools
What You Learn
Child development
Infant, toddler, and school-aged curricula
Language, literacy, and speech disorders
Health, safety and nutrition
Children's math and science
Special education
Introduction to psychology and sociology
Recognized by the College of Early Childhood Educators. Graduates may apply to become Registered Childhood Educators.
