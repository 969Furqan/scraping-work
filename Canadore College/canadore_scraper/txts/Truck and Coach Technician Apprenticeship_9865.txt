Truck and Coach Technician Apprenticeship
Train in a professional atmosphere with other students working in the trade.
Overview
Canadore College has close working relationships within the trucking industry and has strong support from its local repair facilities. The College was integral in the development of this program in conjunction other colleges, with the primary manufacturers and transportation leaders within this industry.
Ontario Certificate of Qualifications are nationally and internationally recognized.
Length of Study:
3 Levels. Each level is one day per week for 36 weeks for a total of 240 hours.
Note:
Upon completion, successful students are eligible to challenge the Ontario Certificate of Qualification.
Your Future Career
Truck and coach technician
Service/Parts manager
Equipment purchaser
Maintenance director
Shop foreperson
What You Learn
Invasive and non-invasive diagnostic techniques
Related computer operations for diagnostic purposes
Interpreting government regulations
