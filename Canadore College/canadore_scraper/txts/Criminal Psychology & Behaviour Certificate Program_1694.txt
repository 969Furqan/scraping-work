Criminal Psychology & Behaviour Certificate Program
Overview
This program will enhance the knowledge and skills of individuals who work or volunteer in corrections, law enforcement, security, womens shelters, human services, youth at risk, or individuals who have a special interest in criminal psychology and behaviour. The program explores why individuals become criminals and explores factors that research suggests underlie criminal behaviour.
Note:
Students have up to 7 years to complete all requirements.
