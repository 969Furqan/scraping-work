Inclusive Leadership Practices: Leadership in Contemporary Workplaces
Overview
This 42 hour course provides students with the opportunity to analyze the evolving nature of contemporary workplaces in an increasingly globalized world. Various models of leadership will be examined with a focus on practices that build effective work environments. Students will explore and reflect on their own values and beliefs relative to leadership practices.
