Correctional Administration I
Overview
This course provides the learner with an understanding of the legal parameters of correctional operations, the organizational framework of federal and provincial correctional services, and an overview of staffing models and responsibilities in secure environments. It is designed as an introductory module to establish a knowledge foundation for subsequent application of security skills.
