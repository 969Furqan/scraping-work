Working in Broadband - 21 hours
Overview
This online course will introduce students to the Rural Broadband Community. Students will gain an understanding of the industry as a whole and explore career pathways, a day in the life in various broadband specific roles and see how to grow a career and make a life in an essential and highly rewarding and transformational industry.  The online course is approximately 21 hours in duration.
