Basic Language Skills
Develop your skills in preparation for further language development
Overview
This beginner level intensive language training program will help you develop skills in preparation for further language development. Courses included reading, writing, grammar and oral skills. Canadore provides quality English language training in a caring and supportive environment using modern and social technologies.
Note:
Credential awarded is Canadore College Certificate – English as a Second Language: Basic Language Skills

Admission Requirements
Admission Requirements
International students must either a) provide a IELTS or equivalent score for placement purposes in the appropriate program or b) complete a placement test at the start of the semester to identify the appropriate program to be placed in. Domestic students will complete a placement test the start of the semester.
