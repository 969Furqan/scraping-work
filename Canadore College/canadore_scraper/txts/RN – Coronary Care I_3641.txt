Overview
Review cardiac anatomy and physiology. Increase your cardiac assessment skills, and improve your knowledge and nursing skills of coronary syndromes. All common arrhythmias are included.

Learning Outcomes
Learning outcomes represent culminating demonstrations of learning and achievement. In addition, learning outcomes are interrelated and cannot be viewed in isolation of one another.
