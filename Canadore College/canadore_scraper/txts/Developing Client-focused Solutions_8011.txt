Developing Client-focused Solutions
Overview
When looking for the ideal product or service, every customer wants to know: WIIFM? (Whats In It For Me?). By applying a customer-focused lens to analyze client needs and deconstruct value propositions, you will be able to build client engagement and agreement and ultimately a viable sales solution. This 12 hour course will provide you with the tools and techniques to solve problems, meet client needs, and present client-focused sales solutions.

Admission Requirements

Learning Outcomes
Learning outcomes represent culminating demonstrations of learning and achievement. In addition, learning outcomes are interrelated and cannot be viewed in isolation of one another. As such, they should be viewed as a comprehensive whole. They describe performances that demonstrate that significant integrated learning by graduates of the program has been achieved.
