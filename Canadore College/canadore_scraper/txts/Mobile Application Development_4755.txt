Mobile Application Development
Get the skills you need to launch your career in the cutting-edge field of mobile app development.
Overview
Upcoming Intake: TBD
Mobile devices are everywhere and are used to readily in the business and personal lives of people all over the world. This field is exploding and skilled app developers are in high demand. New applications are being created daily for games, maps, medical applications, banks, media outlets - and the list goes on. Students work hands on with Blackberry, Playbooks, iPads, and Android devices.

Admissions Requirements
Admissions Requirements
The minimal requirements are one of the following:
Graduate of a recognized two year college program with a strong academic mark
Graduate of a recognized three year university program with a strong academic mark
Potential students should be able to demonstrate their comfort with computer technology. While a programming or a layout and design background is important, it is not essential.
If English is not your first language you must provide proof of English competency.
View current English Language Proficiency Requirements.
