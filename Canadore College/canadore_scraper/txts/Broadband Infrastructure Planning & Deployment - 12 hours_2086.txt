Broadband Infrastructure Planning & Deployment - 12 hours
Overview
This online course will provide a high-level understanding of the importance of developing and implementing a broadband infrastructure in rural, remote and Indigenous regions of Canada.  The intended audience is government managers and officials, urban and land use planners, Indigenous community leaders, and other administrative personnel.   This course will also cover the urgency of the social & economic need for connectivity from an equity, economic and workforce development perspective. The online course is approximately 12 hours in duration.
