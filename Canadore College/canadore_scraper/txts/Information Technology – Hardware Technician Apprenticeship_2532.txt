Information Technology – Hardware Technician Apprenticeship
Overview
Learn the intricacies of microcomputer applications. The entire theory learning portion of this apprenticeship is completed online, while the hands-on training is completed on the job.
Each level takes one and a half years to complete.
Level 1 – 360 hours
Level 2 – 270 hours
Note
: Program Length - 1.5 years per level
Your Future Career:
Microcomputer or mainframe-computing support for:
IT firms
Public corporations
Private corporations
What you Learn:
Microcomputers and microcomputer applications
Operating systems
Desktop and mobile platforms
Customer service and professionalism
Electronics and integrated circuit concepts
