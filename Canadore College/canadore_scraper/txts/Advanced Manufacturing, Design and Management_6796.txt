Advanced Manufacturing, Design and Management
Learn hands-on in this in-demand career field
Overview
Upcoming Intake:
Fall 2025: Commerce Court – North Bay
Canada is facing a critical skills shortage. Skilled workers and leaders are in high demand in the manufacturing sector. This rapidly evolving industry is increasingly relying on the use of technology, automation and robotics. Gain skills and knowledge in manufacturing technologies, digital manufacturing, and advanced computer assisted design tools and techniques that are used by industry.  This program will prepare you to launch your career in advanced manufacturing locally, across Canada or in the global market.
