Accounting Foundations Certificate Program
Overview
The Accounting Certificate Program is ideal for individuals who wish to acquire a strong foundation in accounting. Whether you are looking for accounting experience or for advancement in your professional career, this program provides an intensive and complete curriculum. In order to earn the certificate, students must successfully complete a total of 11 compulsory courses.

Students have up to 7 years to complete all requirements

Admission Requirements

Learning Outcomes
