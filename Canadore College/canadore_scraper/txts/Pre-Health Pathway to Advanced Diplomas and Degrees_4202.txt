Pre-Health Pathway to Advanced Diplomas and Degrees
Prepare hands-on for entrance into and advanced diploma or degree
Overview
Upcoming Intake:
Fall 2024: College Drive – North Bay
This program will provide you with the knowledge, skills and abilities to continue your education in an advanced health sciences program or other science related program at the advanced or degree level. Learn the fundamentals needed to advance your education and career while learning about the vast opportunities and potential careers. Graduates of this program may be eligible for transfer credits into your health or science program of choice.
