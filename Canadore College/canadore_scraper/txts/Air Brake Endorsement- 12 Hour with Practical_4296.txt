Air Brake Endorsement: 12 Hour with Practical
Overview
This Ministry of Transportation-approved course ensures that drivers attain the knowledge necessary to operate brake systems safely. Participants who successfully complete both the written and practical test will receive the documentation, to provide to the Ministry, to obtain the "Z" endorsement classification on their license. Students will be assigned ½ hour appointments, on the final day, to complete the practical test.
