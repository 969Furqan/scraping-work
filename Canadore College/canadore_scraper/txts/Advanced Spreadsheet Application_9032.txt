Advanced Spreadsheet Application
Overview
This course is designed to teach advanced topics of MS Excel. Students will learn how to create and manipulate advanced spreadsheets and be able to manipulate data, work with templates and styles, connect to external data and links, review and protect workbooks, create custom formats and layouts and create advanced formulas. The course will also help to prepare students wishing to pursue the MOS certification exam Excel Expert 2016 Exam 77-728. It is recommended students complete COMP8166 or equivalent before taking this course.
