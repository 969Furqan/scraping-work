Part Time
New Venture Creation (7 weeks)
Overview
In this 56 hour course, students will be exposed to the various aspects of entrepreneurship including, but not limited to, business idea generation, feasibility analysis, creating a business plan, conducting market research, exploring financial structure, and legal aspects of starting a new venture in Canadian context. Students will prepare a business plan to apply the knowledge they will gain in this course.
