Stress, Wellness and Nutrition
Overview
Because stress can play a huge factor in people's lives, learning how to deal with it is crucial to long-term health.  Although stress does not cause disease, it triggers molecular changes throughout the body that make people more susceptible to many illnesses.  This course, will outline both an understanding and importance of how to deal with with both positive and negative stress in our lives.  By using wellness perspectives, each student will gain insight into how they can control both internal and external stressors.
