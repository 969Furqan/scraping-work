Part Time
Payroll Administration
Overview
This course covers the following topics: maintaining payroll records; salaried, hourly, commission and contract workers; taxable benefits, statutory and other deductions; preparation of payroll journal entries; preparation of Records of Employment; preparation of T4s and T4 Summary; Workers’ Compensation; Employment Standards; and Computerized Payroll.
This course is an equivalent for CIB333 in the
Bookkeeping
certificate.
