Gas Technician 2: Piping Fitter
Overview
This course, which is a requirement of the full Gas Technician 2 certification, focusses on topics from Modules 10, 15 and 22 of the Technical Standards and Safety Authority approved curriculum. The course is delivered in-person in our gas lab over one week and is an intensive, hands-on training course. Topics will include advanced piping and tubing systems, gas meters, pressure regulators, relief valves, and venting practices including how to properly install, repair and inspect venting systems. We recommend completing GAS200 prior to registering for this course.
Prerequisite: Gas Technician 3 certificate, GAS200, GAS205
