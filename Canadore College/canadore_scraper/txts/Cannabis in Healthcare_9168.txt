Cannabis in Healthcare
Overview
The regulatory landscape for cannabinoid-based medicine has rapidly changed over the past 3 years, and millions of patients across Canada now have access to legal cannabis for their therapeutic needs. As such, it has become imperative for students and professionals in healthcare and allied health fields to have a working understanding of the best medical practices as it pertains to effective medical cannabis therapy. This course is designed bridge the gap as you engage with patients and health professionals regarding medical cannabis practices and help those pursuing a professional or academic interest in Cannabis as a novel therapeutic medicine.
