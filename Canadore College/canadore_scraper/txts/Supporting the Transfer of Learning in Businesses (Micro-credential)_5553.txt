Supporting the Transfer of Learning in Businesses (Micro-credential)
Overview
In the 21 hour Supporting the Transfer of Learning in Business micro-credential, students explore the definition and application of learning transfer, as well as approaches for supporting new and ongoing business goals and objectives. Emphasis is placed on how to assess the impact of learning and the tools used to incorporate learning transfer through instructional design.

Learning Outcomes
Learning Outcomes
Learning outcomes represent culminating demonstrations of learning and achievement. In addition, learning outcomes are interrelated and cannot be viewed in isolation of one another. As such, they should be viewed as a comprehensive whole. They describe performances that demonstrate that significant integrated learning by graduates of the program has been achieved.
