Wastewater Treatment Certification Level 3 & 4
Overview
The purpose of this course is to present advanced knowledge and practices, theories, and applications relevant to wastewater flows and characteristics, treatment processes, and plant operations. Topics covered in Wastewater Treatment Certification Level I & II are covered in more detail and depth. This will prepare students to write the higher level certification examinations.
