Perioperative Nursing 2
Overview
Surgeries specific to gastrointestinal, liver, pancreas,biliary tract, breast and gynecologic/obstetric.Wound healing is also presented in this course. Discussion includes anatomy, instrumentation, patient care and positioning, special equipment required, post-op care, pain management and information unique to the above surgical topics.
