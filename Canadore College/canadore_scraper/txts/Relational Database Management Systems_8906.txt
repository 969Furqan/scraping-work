Relational Database Management Systems
Overview
Explore building data models, normalization, and querying a database to benefit GIS. Work with spatial data that
is stored in a relational database management system and build on storage techniques using relational databases
(non-spatial) and spatially enabled databases (Geodatabases). You will also navigate security, administration, and
the beginnings of enterprise driven data management.
