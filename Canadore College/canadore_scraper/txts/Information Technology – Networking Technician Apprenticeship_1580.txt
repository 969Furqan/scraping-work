Information Technology – Networking Technician Apprenticeship
Overview
Learn the intricacies of microcomputer applications and advanced networking. The entire theory learning portion of this apprenticeship is completed online, while the hands on training is completed on the job.
Each level takes one and a half years to complete.
Level 1 – 360 hours
Level 2 – 360 hours
Your Future Career:
Microcomputer or mainframe-computing support for:
IT firms
Public Corporations
Private Corporations
What you Learn:
Operating systems
Microcomputer applications
Basic electrical and electronics
Desktop and mobile platforms
Basic network systems
