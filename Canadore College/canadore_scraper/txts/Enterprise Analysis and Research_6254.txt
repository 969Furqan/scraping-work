Enterprise Analysis and Research
Apply what you’ve learned working with a real organization for your final capstone project.
Overview
Upcoming Intake:
Fall 2024: Commerce Court – North Bay
Prepare for an exciting career in one of the fastest growing fields. Data analytics is used in many different sectors including marketing, law enforcement, and community and social services. Gain the skills needed to interpret data and predictive models to successfully give direction to both private and government organizations. This training provides the foundation for a dynamic career in a number of disciplines including cyber security or artificial intelligence-the possibilities are endless.
