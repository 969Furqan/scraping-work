Gas Technician 3 - Practical
Overview
The successful completion of this two-week, in-house practical course provides participants with the required entry-level skills to work under the supervision of a certified Gas Technician 2 or Gas Technician 1 to assist in the installation and servicing of propane and natural gas systems up to an including 400,000 BTU. This course complies with the practical education requirements of the Technical Standards and Safety Authority training curriculum for the Gas Technician 3 certification (modules 1-9).
Students must complete GAS300 and GAS305.
