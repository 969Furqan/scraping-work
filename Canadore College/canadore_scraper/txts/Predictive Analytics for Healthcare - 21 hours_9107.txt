Predictive Analytics for Healthcare - 21 hours
Overview
Predictive Analytics for Healthcare is an introductory online course designed for healthcare professionals and leaders across all disciplines. This course provides a broad understanding of data analytics and its role in enhancing decision-making within healthcare settings. Participants will learn the fundamentals of predictive analytics, explore its practical applications in clinical practice and healthcare policy, and discover how to apply these insights to improve their professional practice.
This course will take approximately 21 hours to complete.
