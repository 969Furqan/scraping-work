Automotive Service Advisor 2
Overview
Gain an overview of the role of automotive service advisor. Learn to identify and maintain high standards of customer relations as well as study sales techniques, interpersonal skills, effective communication, provincial legislation governing auto repair, and the effective use of labor rate guides.

Learning Outcomes
