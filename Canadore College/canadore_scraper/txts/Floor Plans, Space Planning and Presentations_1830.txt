Floor Plans, Space Planning and Presentations
Overview
In this course, students will use methods, concepts, and theories to understand and develop floor plans and read blue prints. Students will be drawing room dimensions and wall elevations to scale, as well as learning to identify blue print symbols. Through hands on assignments and practice, students will be experimenting with furniture layouts, room settings, and creating presentation boards that will enhance knowledge of standard industry measurements and space planning techniques.
