Programs/Courses
Railway Car Technician Apprenticeship
Canadore College is the only college in the province of Ontario to offer this program.
Overview
Canadore College was integral in the development of this program in conjunction with stakeholders and leaders within this industry.
Length of Study:
3 Levels. Each level is an 8 week block, 30 hours per week for a total of 240 hours.
Notes:
Upon completion, successful students are eligible to challenge the Ontario Certificate of Qualification.
Your Future Career
Service/Parts manager
Equipment purchaser
Operations manager
Section foreperson
What You Learn
Personal and Workplace Safety
Invasive and non-invasive diagnostic techniques
Related computer operations for diagnostic purposes
Interpreting government regulations
Job estimating and scheduling
