Helicopter Flight Training
Learn all aspects of helicopter operations
Overview
The partnership between Canadore and Helicopters Canada offers a unique blend of academic, operational and hands-on training. The college-structured classes combined with extensive practical training, both on the ground and in the air, will provide you with the skills, knowledge and attitude necessary to operate helicopters safely and efficiently under various flight conditions and in remote geographical locations. Our graduates are in demand, and the school’s diverse fleet of aircraft, certified training equipment and experienced instructors will help you hone your skills for a range of exciting career opportunities in Canada and across the world.

Certification/Accreditation
Certification/Accreditation
Commercial Helicopter Flight Licence with ratings on the helicopter types specifically trained on. In addition, First Aid Certification, CPR, Winter Survival Certification, Underwater Egress Certification, Pilot Decision Making, and Human Factors Certification.
Students also receive basic helicopter maintenance and will have other opportunities for further certification.
