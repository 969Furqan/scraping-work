Overview
Upcoming Intake:
Fall 2024: Commerce Court – North Bay
Technician - 2 Years / Technology - 3 Years
Become skilled in this in-demand and important field. Learn to design, develop and maintain machine components, tools, heating and ventilation systems, power generation and manufacturing plants and equipment, and much more. Mechanical engineering technicians and technologists are sought after in a number of industries including mining, manufacturing, and forestry.
