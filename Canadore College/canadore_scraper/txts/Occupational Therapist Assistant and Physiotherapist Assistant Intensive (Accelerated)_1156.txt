Occupational Therapist Assistant and Physiotherapist Assistant Intensive (Accelerated)
Several placement opportunities in settings such as hospitals, private clinics and home care
Overview
Upcoming Intake:
Fall 2024: College Drive – North Bay
University degree graduates from a related field of study can complete this highly collaborative program in 10 months. OTAs and PTAs work with people who have limited or impaired functionality due to injury, illness, disability or aging. This program is designed for students looking for OTA/PTA designation and for those who want to enhance their application to a Master of Physical Therapy, Physiotherapy or Occupational Therapy. Students train through simulation, experiential learning, interprofessional learning and over 500 placement hours under the guidance and supervision of registered occupational therapists and physiotherapists.
