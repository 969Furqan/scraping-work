Overview
Upcoming Intake:
Fall 2025: College Drive – North Bay
Get the advanced knowledge and skills you need to work in the rapidly growing and evolving field of
bioinformatics
.  Learn to analyze scientific data, perform data mining, and monitor emerging trends. Develop the methods and tools to understand and disseminate complex biological data. Graduate with work-ready skills and global career opportunities in a variety of sectors including pharmaceutical, environmental, agricultural, genomics, and much more.
Your Future Career:
Bioinformatics technician
Scientific data analyst
Data scientist
Biostatistician
Computational geneticist
Computational Molecular Scientist

Bioconductor and Biopython

Admission Requirements
Admission Requirements
Ontario College Diploma, Ontario College Advanced Diploma, Degree, or equivalent in biological sciences, biotechnology, or a related program.
If English is not your first language you must provide proof of English competency.
