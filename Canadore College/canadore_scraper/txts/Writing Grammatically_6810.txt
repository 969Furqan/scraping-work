Writing Grammatically
Overview
This course will allow for students to learn the rules of English grammar in an easy-to-follow manner.  This course will begin with a review of sentence parts, followed by an in-depth explanation of sentence structuring, and culminates in a practical review of stylistic conventions in both business and professional writing.
