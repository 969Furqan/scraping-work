IT jobs are experiencing rapid growth, particularly in the automobile, medical technology, and wireless communication industries. computer and information technology
.

information technology

What can you do with an information technology degree?
The U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS) projects strong growth for IT jobs over the next decade, with some positions expected to expand by as much as 32% by 2032. Additionally, the BLS reports a median IT salary of about $104,420 per year, significantly higher than the national average for all jobs.
